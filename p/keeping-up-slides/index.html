Navigating a technology disruption

When a powerful technology disrupts what we do, the first instinct is damage control: the new tool hurts our old way of working, so we try to protect it. The opportunity is the opposite — fighting fire with fire: we use that same technology to do things we never could before. Fear first, the good stuff later. AI at Kellogg is my clearest example of the good stuff.
