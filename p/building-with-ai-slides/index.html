Not everything about AI is good.
So why am I encouraging you to use it?
Say it out loud, the slide stays minimal. It's a powerful technology that can do harm, make learning harder, and shake the status quo. We always see the negative first: a new tool arrives, people think "replace these people, change these jobs," and that feels bad because what we knew stops working. Fair. But there's real upside too, and the only way to feel it is to try, to think creatively, to do things we simply couldn't before. Not AI replacing us, AI moving us forward. Pivot straight into the next slide.
Let's get building

Why start building now?

It builds on what you know
There are no AI experts, and you can reach the frontier fast.
You're not starting from scratch. AI magnifies the expertise you already have, call it 95% you, 5% AI. Huge payoff for very little.
It puts you in control
The more you use it, the better you avoid the real trap: letting AI think for you. Use it to learn, not to replace your thinking.
And you get a seat at the table, helping decide where this all goes.
The timing is perfect
An MBA gives you the time, the people, and the network to explore and build together.
You're never behind. What I knew six months ago barely matters now.
And interviews already ask one thing: "What have you built with AI?"
Three reasons, the arc Sébastien wants. (1) empowering: no experts, reach the frontier fast, AI magnifies YOUR expertise, drop the imposter syndrome. (2) control: use it to learn not to think for you (the Kellogg pitfall), and you get a seat at the table in where AI goes. (3) timing: an MBA is built for this, and you're never late because AI expertise gets obsolete fast. Keep it brisk.
Let's get building

What you can do now

  • Download Claude Cowork or OpenAI Codex. You won't regret it.
  • Pick one real task and just try it. Give it your files, talk to it, keep going.
  • Do a little every day. It adds up faster than you'd think.
  • Stuck? Ask the AI, lean on the Kellogg AI Club, or build with a classmate. You'll be going within a week.
Actionable before philosophy. They're about to have time and a cohort, perfect conditions to start. The job-interview hook now lives on the previous slide.
